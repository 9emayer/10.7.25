South Side Tradition Since 1942

Leon's neon-lit stand has served as a neighborhood beacon for more than eighty years, attracting classic car clubs, families, and late-night snackers. The menu remains refreshingly simple with an emphasis on custard made fresh throughout the day.

Visitors can expect generous portions, speedy service, and a sense of nostalgia that feels straight out of the 1950s. The custard pairs perfectly with a drive down Historic Highway 41 or a stroll through nearby Jackson Park on warm summer nights.

How to Make the Most of Your Visit

  1. Check the flavor board for the day's specials as soon as you park.
  2. Order a turtle sundae and a side of crunchy butter pecans.
  3. Enjoy your custard beneath the glowing marquee lights.

Explore Leon's history and menu at leonsfrozencustard.us, and bring cash for their famously efficient walk-up windows.
