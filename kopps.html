Modern Flavors, Iconic Burgers

Kopp's expansive custard stands draw crowds for both towering sundaes and savory butter burgers. Daily rotating flavors are announced online and can include everything from tiramisu to lemon blueberry shortbread.

Each suburban location features airy patios, lush landscaping, and plenty of space to linger with friends. Grab a flavor-of-the-day pint to take home while enjoying the famous "squeaky" Wisconsin cheese curds on-site.
